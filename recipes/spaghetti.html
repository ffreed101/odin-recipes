Spaghetti

noodle meat sauce goods

This is a basic spaghetti that anyone with a pot, a stove, and about 7 dollars can make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Meat
  • Pack of Thin Spaghetti
  • Any Spaghetti Sauce of your choosing. Hell, even if you make and can your own you can use it.
  • Toppings(Cheese, peppers, seasoning, etc. its really up to you)

Instructions

  1. Set a pot of water to eventually boil.
  2. Get a cooking pot or deep pan and start heating it to brown the ground meat.
  3. Stir Occasionally making sure all the meat gets nice and brown. Feel free to season at this point.
  4. Once meat is brown, strain the oil in a strainer bowl and return to the pot with sauce.
  5. Once the water you set starts boiling, pour in your pasta and stir intensively until it returns to a boil.
  6. Allow your sauce to simmer while checking on the tenderness of the pasta.
  7. When the pasta is cooked to your desire, pour MOST of the water out. The starch in the boiled water keeps the noodles from sticking together.
  8. Assemble pasta and add toppings. Enjoy!
